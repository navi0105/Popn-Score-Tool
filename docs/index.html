Usage

  1. Log in to Jam&Fizz on e-amusement (requires Basic Course)
  2. Click Pop'n Score Tool in your bookmarks bar
  3. Click Scrape to fetch your scores
  4. Click Export HTML for an offline viewer, or Export Image for a shareable PNG
